Purchase Consulting Services

Contact info@ardisialabs.com to discuss and/or purchase consulting services.

In your email, please indicate the following:

  • The name of your organization.
  • A description of what services you are requesting (help with Library integration, UX design, etc.).
  • Whether or not your organization has purchased licenses for the Ardisia Component Library.

Consulting services are generally sold and valued in terms of how many hours of Ardisia Labs's time is being purchased. If you know the number of hours you would like to purchase, indicate as much in your email.
