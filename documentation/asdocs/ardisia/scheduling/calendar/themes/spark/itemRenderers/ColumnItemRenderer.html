Classpublic class ColumnItemRenderer
InheritanceColumnItemRenderer mx.core.UIComponent
Implements IColumnRenderer

Pure actionscript column item renderer designed for use by the Calendar class. Much faster than MXML defined renderers.

Do not not forget to provide a default minHeight in measure() for custom renderers.
