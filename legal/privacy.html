Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) for Ardisia Labs (“Ardisia”, "us", "we", or "our") describes our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Services and the choices you have associated with that information.

For the purposes of this Policy, “Services” refers to our website, our software products, and any support offered on those products. “Personal Information” refers to any information that can be used to identify a person.

Information You Provide To Us

We will have any information you submit to us directly via email.

When users purchase any of our products, users are forwarded to a third-party payment processor which will ask you for the personal information necessary to validate the transaction. Data such as name, email, paypal or credit card, address, etc. After purchase, you will receive an email at the email address you provided during purchasing with a request that you email us your Github account name. This is used to provide you with access to the product.

Any financial details you submit to the payment processor are not available to us or stored by us. Other personal information you submit during the purchase transaction, such as address, name, etc., will be made available to us by the payment processor so we can identify the licensee and provide the Services.

Purchasers will have access to the product via Github for a specified amount of time This is also the avenue for support questions. Your Github account name and any information you publicly share on your Github profile will be visible to us and other users of the repository. Any personal information you include in questions or issues you submit in the repository are visible to us and other users. This is standard behavior for Github repositories.

Automatically Collected Information

We do not use cookies, web tracking software, or requests for personal information on visitors to our website. Third-party partners, such as the payment processor, and the download/support.html channel providers may. See below for information on third party partners.

How we use the information

We use the collected information to provide purchasers of our product with access to the product, an avenue for support, to communicate with you about the Services, to prevent fraud, and other uses consistent with providing the product and Services.

We need personal information during the purchase transaction to associate purchasers with owners of legitimate licenses. Also, personal information is required to provide support.

Who Do We Share The Information With

We do not sell user data or have a practice of disseminating personal information.

Sharing Information With Github Users

When you use our Services to download the product or seek assistance, your Github user name and profile may be visible to other users of the Services. If this is a problem, update your Github profile to not include any personal information. Also, any personal information you submit via the issue tracker for support will be visible to other purchasers who have access to the repository.

Sharing Information With Third-Party Providers

We use a third party hosting service to host the website. Even though we do not seek to collect any IP addresses, geographic data, or other web analytic data, the hosting company may collect such data as a part of its standard operating procedure and to prevent abuse and fraud. Ardisia does not store such data or analyze it. Ardisia would only seek to use such data in the event of fraud or abuse, and only for short term logs. We are not seeking to collect personal information on our website.

Forced Disclosure

We may use or disclose your Personal Information if required by law or if we reasonably believe that use or disclosure is necessary to protect ourselves, others, prevent fraud, any other legal process.

Storage and Security

Our website does not store any personal information. We leave storage and processing to established and trusted third party companies that specialize in storage and provide up-to-date services. See the section below labeled “Ardisia Subprocessors” for more information on the third party partners we use.

Storage Duration

We retain your personal information for a period consistent with the original purpose of collection. For instance, we may retain your personal information during the time you have an account to use the Services, and for a reasonable period thereafter. We also may retain your personal information during the period needed for Ardisia to pursue legitimate business interests, conduct audits, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Right to access and update information

If you would like to update any information on yourself, please email us at info@ardisialabs.com and we will update it for you to the best of our ability. If the update relates to your Github account, use Github to update your information.

Right to delete your information

If you would like us to delete any information on yourself, please email us at info@ardisialabs.com and we will delete it to the best of our ability. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and notify you of the action we have taken. If the delete relates to your Github account, use Github to delete your information.

Right to restrict information processing

In some cases, you may ask us to stop accessing, storing, using and otherwise processing your information where you believe we don't have the appropriate rights to do so. To exercise this right, please email us at info@ardisialabs.com

Right to data portability

If you would like us to deliver to you any information we have collected on you, email info@ardisialabs.com. We will provide you with an electronic file of the information. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and notify you of the action we have taken.

How we transfer the information we collect internationally

Any information collected is stored by sub-processors located in the United States. See below.

Age limitations

To the extent prohibited by law, Ardisia does not solicit, seek, or allow the use of the Services by anyone younger than 16 years old. If you learn that anyone younger than 16 has unlawfully provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your information is handled, please contact info@ardisialabs.com
